    Method
    1. Heat a wok or a large skillet over high heat until very hot
      2.
    2. Add 1 tablespoon of oil, and then the broccoli, carrots, cabbage
      3.
    3. Stir often until the vegetables are crisp-tender, about 4 minutes
      4.
    4. Transfer them to a plate and set aside
      5.
    5. Add the remaining tablespoon of oil, then add the cooled rice
      6.
    6. Stir fry until it is lightly browned in spots, about 5 minutes
      7.
    7. Add the ginger and the garlic to the rice, and stir
      8.
    8. Return the vegetables to the wok with the rice
      9.
    9. Cook stirring often, until everything is combined
      10.
    10. Taste and adjust the seasonings with salt and pepper
      11.
    11. Garnish and serve
